Our Mission: Support our active-duty military and coalition forces, K9/handler teams by providing them items that they need and will boost their morale. By donating money or signing up to send a care package, you're helping ensure our military and coalition forces get the love and appreciation from home they deserve!

Heroes Betterment Inc. (formerly HB Care Packages) was formed in 2012 and became a 501(c)3 Federal Non-profit in 2016. Heroes Betterment has sent over 3000 carepackages, (occasionally receiving requests for units of up to 120 personnel) and currently has over 80 actively deployed K9 teams and 60 deployed service-members.
